Infantry

Infantry units can all be built at cities, they tend to be the core of an army as they are very versatile and have a constant, steady movement. They also have the added bonus of being able to build improvements such as trenches, fences, and bunkers. Infantry is unable to properly defend against cavalry (without a fence) and is able to deal high damage to artillery.

Militia

"Troop constituted by civilians. Quite weak." And terrible defense capabilities make this units combat usefulness run thin, as its only real advantages are that it is cheap and can be created at any city as has the least requirements.

Uses

  • Damage reducer, the infantry will take the damage and not the artillery behind it.
  • Spamming to hold the level VI and VII cities any turn.

Statistics

  • Damage: 1-5, 3-6,1-6
  • Health: 60-90-110
  • Range: 1
  • Food consumption: 4-8-12
  • Movement: 6

Line Infantry

"Trained infantry. Main force of land troops." Despite what the game says, there are more effective units at a similar cost. These troops are very helpful for support/defense.

Uses

  • Cheap tanker, it can survives many attacks to hold the defensive line.

Statistics

  • Damage: 1-6
  • Health: 80-120-145
  • Range: 1
  • Food consumption: 5-10-15
  • Movement: 6

Light Infantry

"Maintain high movement in various terrains. High shot accuracy, low defense."

Uses

  • Higher movement than regular infantry

Statistics

  • Damage: 3-6
  • Health: 70-105-125
  • Range: 1
  • Food consumption: 4-8-12
  • Movement: 8

Grenadier

"Constituted by well-built infantries. Strong capability. Can attack still target with grenades." Note: If you have a lot of resources, don't use grenadier, use guards instead.

Uses

  • Attack and hold the line with cheaper cost than guards.
  • Destroy remain fortresses or ships from the enemy.

Statistics

  • Damage: 2-7
  • Health: 75-115-135
  • Range: 1
  • Food consumption: 5-10-15
  • Movement: 6

Guards

"Elite infantry with high offense and defense. Can attack still target with grenades." Basically the grenadier with more health, damage and more expensive. Note: The third type of guards can only be built at level VII cities.

Uses

  • Attack and defence effectively. Infantry generals should be put on Guards, try to train tier 3 guards for generals if you as it gives them an extra 30 health.
  • Destroy fortresses and ships at maximum health.

Statistics

  • Damage: 3-8
  • Health: 90-135-165
  • Range: 1
  • Food consumption: 6-12-18
  • Movement: 6

Machine Gun

"The new weapon can deal deadly strike to infantry can cavalry with continuous fire. Movement is quite low." A weapon from the future. Not extremely effective without a general due to low movement, health, and vulnerability to artillery.

Uses

  • Destroy cavalry and infantry perfectly. Capable of killing enemy generals. Be aware of artillery attack!
  • Can hold the line from infantry and cavalry attacks effectively

Statistics

  • Damage: 2-10
  • Health: 75-115
  • Range: 1
  • Food consumption: 6-12
  • Movement: 5
