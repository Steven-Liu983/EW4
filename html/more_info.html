More Information

Taverns

Taverns are often located in cities in conquest mode and can let you recruit generals for temporary use. They cost less than 10 medals but you need to use some money and wrenches. You must recruit or place some soldiers in the city in order to recruit the general. There is round limit where you can recruit this general only after the round is reached. This general can only be used in this conquest mode and cannot be found in your headquarters.

Trading

Trading for generals is an aspect along with infantry, cavalry, artillery and navy. It can be used in shops and trading posts. Trading posts is often located in cities and generals' trading can increase the supplies traded.

  • Trading 0 star: x3.0
  • Trading 1 star: x2.2
  • Trading 2 star: x2.2
  • Trading 3 star: x1.8
  • Trading 4 star: x1.4
  • Trading 5 star: x1.0

Shop

A shop is located in headquarters and sometimes in cities in campaign mode. It sells stars, boost packs and general equipment. It can be bought using medals and can be affected by generals' trading stars.

  • Trading 1 star: -0%
  • Trading 2 star: -8%
  • Trading 3 star: -12%
  • Trading 4 star: -16%
  • Trading 5 star: -20%
