Generals' Skills

Combat statistics modifier skills

  • Explosive (artillery), Surprise (cavalry), Formation (infantry): increases maximum damage by 1
  • Ballistic (artillery), Mobility (cavalry), Infantry tactics (infantry): increases minimum damage by 1

Combat non-statistics modifier skills

  • Defensive art: reduces all damage received from an attack to 1 (~20% chance to occur)
  • Assault art: inflict maximum damage (~20% chance to occur)
  • Siege master: inflict an additional 15% damage to units in town
  • Bugle (infantry), Strike (cavalry), Accurate (artillery): reduces the "evasion" of the unit you are attacking
  • Mass Fire: always deals full damage regardless of current health (only on infantry)
  • Leadership: morale cannot be reduced by being encircled or out of food or due to a cavalry charge
  • Banner: allows general to equip "Banner" items
  • Spy: large 50% bonus damage against fortifications only
  • Fireproof: prevents fires from occurring in the location the general is stationed
  • Steersman: reduces damage (10%) received from enemy ships' attack

Utility skills

  • Geography: ignores the effect of terrain
  • Engineering: artillery movement +2
  • Navigation: ship movement +1
  • Nobleman: increase the experience of the rank of nobility
  • War Expert: increase the experience of military rank
  • War Master: increase the experience of military rank by 80%
  • Trainer: used for regrouping (permanantly removing 1 general to to add 1 star onto another general)
  • Economic Expert: gets 40% more resource in the location the general is stationed
  • Economic Master: gets 80% more resource in the location the general is stationed
  • Architecture: cheaper to build structures (cities, forts, etc.)
  • Logistic: unit does not need food supply
  • Trench/Fence/Bunker/Carrier: money required to build trench/fence/bunker/carrier reduced by 50%
