Princesses

Princesses are achieved by winning conquest in either Europe or America. When you win a conquest game in less than 100 rounds in Europe or America, you get "years" ruled in Europe or America and achieve European and American princesses. When you finish a game in 55 rounds or below, you can choose to march to Asia instead, and achieve Asian princesses.

The princesses are a type of elite general, whose ability scales between tier 2 generals to some of the best generals in the game. The first princess unlocked, at winning in Europe in 65 years, or ruling 250 years in Europe, is Sophia. As shown above, Sophia is an excellent and versatile general, capable of being a good general on any troop type, and Isabela can become one of the best generals for artillery (just behind Moreau and ahead a little of Grouchy) in the game if upgraded to 5-star artillery (currently 2-star, but has 3 abilities relevant to Artillery).

There are a total of 8 princesses, all from different nations, in which 2 are from Asia, 1 is from America, and 5 are from Europe. All of them are specified in one of the 8 different areas (Infantry, Cavalry, Artillery, Navy, Fortresses, Trading (Business), Movement and Rank-up).
