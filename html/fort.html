Fortress

Forts are useful for defending cities and borders. However, due to the amount of time it takes to build them they are not always very helpful. Coastal forts on the other hand, can cripple ships and fleets.

Small Fortress

"Temporary wooden fortress. Can hardly resist attack of artillery. Take 2 turns to build."

Uses

  • Build behind enemy units to decrease enemy's morale
  • Use as meat shield to protect other units(For some reason the AI always target the fortress first)

Statistics

  • Damage: 1-7
  • Health: 160
  • Range: 1
  • Food consumption: 0
  • Movement: 0

Fortress

"Solid fortress equipped with cannons. Difficult to conquer. Take 3 turns to build."

Uses

  • Fortress takes a lot of time to be built and costs a lot of money. Plan ahead before building this.

Statistics

  • Damage: 2-8
  • Health: 220
  • Range: 2
  • Food consumption: 0
  • Movement: 0

Large Fortress

"Large and solid fortress constructed with most advanced technology, equipped with new cannons. Take 4 turns to build."

Uses

  • Large Fortress takes a lot of time to be built and costs a lot of money. Plan ahead before building this.

Statistics

  • Damage: 3-9
  • Health: 300
  • Range: 2
  • Food consumption: 0
  • Movement: 0

Coastal Fort

"Can deal great damage to enemy battleship but not suitable to attack land troops. Take 3 turns to build."

Uses

  • Built for protecting the coast. They can also be useful in later campaign missions, where you could lure enemy generals' ship to the coastal fort to deal deadly strike to them.

Statistics

  • Damage: 1-11
  • Health: 90
  • Range: 2
  • Food consumption: 0
  • Movement: 0
